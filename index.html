Why Computer Science?

Ever since I was a young boy, I used electronics all my life. Whether that was schoolwork, video games, cell phones, or other things. I was always fascinated by it and especially software because I had no clue how people created what we use every day and how they maintain it or make updates to it. One day in 2020, my brother (who was a garbageman at the time) came home with a PC that was being thrown out and decided to give it to me for my 18th birthday. I called a friend of my brother's to teach me about computer parts, software, how everything works together, and more. I then got into the world of PC parts, which sent me down a rabbithole and then I started to learn about software. It was intruiging to me and I decided, in my freshman year, to change my major from marketing to computer science. Nowadays, nothing feels better than all of my code runs smoothly and I create something from scratch. I would love to create something that I am passionate about and can help or be enjoyed by millions of people across the globe, and that is what I hope to do in the future, whether that is web development or software engineering.
